Who Are We

Since 2024, Xcess Food Donation System has been dedicated to preventing food waste by partnering with hostels across Kasargod. We collect surplus food that would otherwise be discarded and distribute it to orphanages, food banks, and old age homes, ensuring nutritious meals reach those in need. To date, we've rescued over 10,000 pounds of quality surplus food, promoting sustainability and compassion in our community. Our team, experienced in food safety and operations, is committed to making a lasting impact by transforming excess food into opportunities for nourishment and support.

What We Do

At Xcess Food Donation System, we manage a robust food donation program aimed at preventing food waste and supporting those in need across Kasargod. Our approach involves partnering with hostels to collect surplus food, which we then distribute to orphanages, food banks, and old age homes. We facilitate this process by linking donors with existing non-profit hunger relief organizations, ensuring that excess food reaches those who need it most. Through efficient communication and a dedicated reporting network, we oversee the development of food handling standards, tax valuation, donation reporting, and ongoing program monitoring. Our funding model is sustainable, derived from a portion of our donor partners' tax savings, which enables us to coordinate and expand our food donation efforts without competing for traditional funding sources. Currently, we are expanding our reach and impact, aiming to establish Harvest Programs with major hostel chains across Kasargod.
